I was born and raised in [Vicenza, Italy]{:target="\_blank"}, a world heritage site known for its Palladian architecture. After completing my scientific studies, I moved to Trento, Italy, where in 2017 I graduated with honors in computer science. During the studies I've worked as a [teaching assistant]{:target="\_blank"}, in a design studio based in Modena, Italy, and in the IT directorate at the University of Trento. There I started my PhD studies in natural language processing.

Some curiosities about me:
- In my freetime I enjoy photography.
- One of my ultimate goals is to learn to communicate science (I'll keep a blog one day!).
- When I was 14, I developed a popular play by chat role-playing medieval game in Italy.
- I have played the electric guitar in a metalcore band.
